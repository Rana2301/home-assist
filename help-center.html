Popular Questions

How do I track my service professional?

Once a professional is assigned, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and email to see their real-time location.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 4 hours before the scheduled time are free. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

Are the service professionals background-checked?

Yes, every HomeAssist professional undergoes a strict identity and criminal background verification process.

How can I pay for my service?

We accept all major UPI apps, credit/debit cards, and cash after the service is completed.

What if I am unhappy with the service?

We offer a 7-day service guarantee. If you aren't satisfied, we will send a pro to re-do the job for free.

Do you offer services outside of Bharuch?

Currently, we are exclusively serving the Bharuch district to ensure the highest quality of local care.
